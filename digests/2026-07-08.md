## Platform & Anthropic News

- **Claude Cowork expands to mobile and web** (July 7) — previously desktop-only; now supports remote sessions, synced files, and a shared Chat/Cowork home across devices, starting with the Max plan. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/07/the-coding-agent-wars-are-spilling-into-the-rest-of-the-office-claude-cowork/)
- **Microsoft 365 write tools** (reported this week) — Claude can now draft/send email, manage calendar events, and create/update OneDrive/SharePoint files, moving beyond read-only M365 access. [blog.mean.ceo](https://blog.mean.ceo/anthropic-claude-news-july-2026/)
- **Claude for Government Desktop — public beta** (reported this week) — Claude Code and Cowork now in beta inside the FedRAMP High authorized Government Desktop app, with stronger admin controls, tamper-evident audit logs, and agency spend governance. [blog.mean.ceo](https://blog.mean.ceo/anthropic-claude-news-july-2026/)
- **J-lens interpretability tool** (reported this week) — new Anthropic research technique ("Jacobian lens") for inspecting internal neural patterns tied to words Claude may use later — described as surfacing "what Claude is thinking but not saying." Research-stage, not a shipped product feature. [EdTech Innovation Hub](https://www.edtechinnovationhub.com/news/anthropic-says-new-j-lens-tool-can-reveal-what-claude-is-thinking-but-not-saying)
